born 1943. Art on display at The Museum of Modern Art, Baltimore Museum of Art, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art, and more



In the early 1907s, Hammons made body prints by coating himself (and others) in grease, pressing the flesh against paper and adding black pigment. This catapulted Hammons into the limelight of the New York art scene.
