Final


For my final project, I have created a piece that demonstrates a topic that I tend 
to be afraid to discuss. For over a decade I have struggled with depression, as well 
as some other mental health struggles such as anxiety and PTSD.

My goal is to tell an abstract narrative about mental health and what happens when 
different factors affect a person with mental health struggles. I want this project 
to be a reflection of my own struggles with mental health. I hope shed a light on how 
there are so many factors that can lead to a person’s struggle with mental health. 
And sometimes a person suffers when there are no environmental stresses.

My project is based on a choose your own adventure book, but in the form of an interactive 
webpage. The user is able to manipulate the video that plays by pressing their choice of 
three buttons. The main page displays a physical, nondescript structure that is sitting 
on a table. Some of the button options are “Water”, “Do Nothing”, and “Fire”. The water 
represents inner turmoil within a person, the do nothing represents ignoring the problems, 
and the fire represents a traumatic life event. The meaning behind this project is how mental 
illness can affect a person both mentally and physically. 

In creating this project, I used a variety of my skills as a multidisciplinary artist. 
I illustrated the animations, created scenes using Adobe After Effects, and coded webpages 
using HTML and Bootstrap.
