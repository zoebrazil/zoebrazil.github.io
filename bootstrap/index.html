Bio


Zoe Brazil was born in Sacramento, California in 1997. She has lived in Sacramento, California, Santa Fe, New Mexico, and currently resides in San José, California.

Zoe attended St. Francis High School in Sacramento from 2011-2015 where she began her journey as an artist and designer. Zoe was an active member of St. Francis High School's theater program where she learned welding, set design, lighting design, and script writing. During her time at St. Francis High School, Zoe also took a variety of arts related classes, including performing arts, theater shop, drawing, art basics, and dance. Zoe was awarded the Academic Achievement Award for Fine Arts and Patrons Award for Technical Arts in 2015.

Zoe then moved to New Mexico for Film Production study at Santa Fe University of Art and Design. Zoe gained hands on production experience in roles such as director, producer, location manager, and prop assistant. Zoe began to discover her passion for design at Santa Fe University of Art and Design when she took classes that furthered her knowledge of Adobe software. Unfortunately the university closed.

Zoe continued her educational journey at Sacramento City College. She took classes to strengthen her skills in fine arts, graphic design, and photography. Zoe graduated with an Associate's in Arts degree for Interdisciplinary Studies: Humanities and Arts in December of 2018.

Zoe has since been attending San José State University for a Bachelor's in Arts degree for Design Studies: Graphic Design. She has taken advertising, business, digital media arts, graphic design, jewelry: small metal smithing, and painting classes to strengthen her understanding as an artist, creator, and designer. Zoe plans to graduate with her degree in Spring 2021.
